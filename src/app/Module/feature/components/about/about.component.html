About The Maheshwar.....

Our mission is to bring the elegance of Maheshwari handloom to the world while supporting traditional artisans.
Every piece we create is woven with heritage and craftsmanship.

Maheshwar is a town, near Khargone city in Khargone district of Madhya Pradesh state, in central India. It is located on State Highway-38 (Khargone city-Barwaha- Bandheri Highway),13.5 km east of National Highway 3 (Agra-Mumbai highway) and 91 km from Indore, the commercial capital of the state. The Town lies on the north bank of the Narmada River. It was the kingdom of Chaktavartin Samrat Sahastraarjun, Kartavirya Arjuna a Heheya king. Lately, after many years, it was the capital of the Malwa during the Maratha Holkar reign till 6 January 1818, when the capital was shifted to Indore by Malhar Rao Holkar III.

The Weaving Process.....

Maheshwari handloom sarees are a true reflection of tradition and craftsmanship. The process starts with carefully choosing the best silk and cotton threads, which are then dyed by hand using age-old techniques. Skilled weavers bring the fabric to life, working with precision and care. The shuttle moves smoothly through the loom, creating elegant patterns that carry the legacy of generations. Every saree is woven with dedication, keeping the rich heritage alive.

Handloom Sarees of Madhya Pradesh

Madhya Pradesh, known as the "heart of India," has a rich tradition of handloom weaving. The state is renowned for its exquisite handloom sarees, especially the Maheshwari sarees. Maheshwari sarees originated in the town of Maheshwar, located on the banks of the Narmada River. These sarees are characterized by their unique designs, vibrant colors, and lightweight fabric. The weavers of Madhya Pradesh have mastered the art of weaving intricate motifs and patterns, inspired by nature, temples, and geometric shapes. The use of pure silk and cotton yarns adds to the elegance and comfort of these sarees. The Maheshwari sarees are known for their distinctive pallu, which features reversible borders and a striking combination of colors.
